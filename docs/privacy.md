# Kordent — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 25, 2026

Kordent ("we", "us") is a stock analysis platform for Indian retail investors, accessible at kordent.in. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and how you can control it.

---

## What We Collect

| Data | How we get it | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Name** | You enter it at sign-up, or we receive it from Google if you sign in with Google | To identify your account and personalize reports |
| **Email address** | You enter it, or we receive it from Google | Account login, portfolio alerts, and daily tracker emails |
| **Portfolio data** (stocks, shares, SIP amounts, sectors, scores, review history) | You create it through the app | To run analysis, track performance, and generate alerts |

We do **not** collect payment information, government IDs, biometric data, or location data.

If you sign in with Google, we only request your **name and email**. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, contacts, calendar, or any other Google service.

---

## How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Kordent service:

- Running stock analysis and portfolio health checks
- Generating daily tracker emails with your portfolio metrics
- Storing your portfolios and score history so they persist across sessions
- Improving the accuracy of our scoring and alert systems

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers. We do **not** use your data for profiling or targeted advertising.

---

## Third-Party Services

Kordent relies on the following services to operate. Your data is shared with them only to the extent needed for the service to function:

| Service | What they receive | Their role | Privacy policy |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Supabase** (Austria/US) | Name, email, portfolio data | Database hosting and authentication | [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy) |
| **Google Gemini API** | Portfolio holdings and market data (no name or email) | AI-powered stock analysis and health check narratives | [ai.google.dev/terms](https://ai.google.dev/terms) |
| **Google OAuth** | Email and name (shared back to us by Google during sign-in) | Account authentication | [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| **yfinance** | Stock ticker symbols only (no user data) | Market price data | — |

**Important:** When you use the chat analysis or health check features, your portfolio holdings (stock names, quantities, prices) are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the analysis. Your name and email are **not** included in these API calls.

---

## Data Storage and Retention

Your data is stored in Supabase (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL). We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Score history is retained indefinitely to enable future trend analysis.

If you delete your account, we will delete all your personal data and portfolio data within 30 days.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data — your portfolios, holdings, and score history are visible in the app at all times
- **Correct** your data — you can update your name and portfolio details through the app
- **Delete** your data — contact us at the email below to request full account and data deletion
- **Withdraw consent** — you can stop using the service at any time; for email alerts, contact us to unsubscribe

These rights apply under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

---

## Security

We use HTTPS for all data in transit, Supabase's built-in encryption for data at rest, and row-level security policies to ensure users can only access their own data. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth (bcrypt-hashed passwords, secure token management).

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a vulnerability, please contact us.

---

## Children

Kordent is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we learn that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.
